Music creates social cohesion, it speaks to all when words can fail, and wherever you go in the world, it is understood.
Music is a universal gift and its power to connect people is without question. It is an art form with human interaction at its centre. Music has the ability to bring people together by the instruments they play, a shared interest in a particular style of music
and who doesn’t love the spine-tingling sounds of a huge orchestral crescendo? We all have been known to move our feet irresistibly
to the rhythm of a drum beat or singing our hearts out to encourage our favourite sports team!
