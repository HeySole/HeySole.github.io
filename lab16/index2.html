Interesting Facts About Princess Diana

  • Prince Charles' 1994 television interview with Jonathan Dimbleby drew a British audience of 14 million viewers. Diana's 1994 television interview on BBC drew 21 million viewers.
  • Diana was on the cover of Time magazine eight times, Newsweek seven times, and People magazine more than 50 times. When she was on the cover of a magazine, sales soared.
  • She grew up with two sisters and two brothers. The siblings were close in childhood.
